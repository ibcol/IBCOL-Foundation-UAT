IBCOL Foundation (International Blockchain Olympiad)

We are an EU non-profit (80637817) combatting bad actors and scams in the “blockchain industry”, i.e. digital trust and web3, and promoting practical solutions to foster a global community of practitioners and to empower young people through education so that they make informed career choices.
