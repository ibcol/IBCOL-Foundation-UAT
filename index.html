The IBCOL Foundation (organiser of the International Blockchain Olympiad) is an EU-based non-profit organisation that supports the development of practical blockchain solutions and growing a global community of practical blockchain practitioners, where blockchain also includes cryptography and web3 overall. The mission is to bring out the latent potential and power of possibility through programs and infrastructure for education in blockchain by helping young people find purpose by introducing blockchain in breadth and depth so that they may make informed decisions on their careers. The impetus for IBCOL Foundation's mission is the prevalence of bad actors and scam projects in the blockchain space.
